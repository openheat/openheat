# Trademarks and Brand Use

The Open Heat Initiative is a community driven, open source effort.  
The content of this repository is licensed under Creative Commons.

Names, logos, and other brand identifiers are **not** covered by the Creative Commons license.

## Name and Marks

The following identify the Open Heat Initiative:

- "Open Heat"
- “Open Heat Initiative”
- “Open Heat Community Code”
- Associated logos, banners, and visual branding
- Official domains, websites, and social accounts

These identifiers may not be used in a way that implies endorsement, sponsorship, or official affiliation without permission.

## Stewardship

Until a dedicated legal structure exists, **Firehub, LLC serves as the administrative steward** of Open Heat Initiative brand assets, including domains and trademarks.

This stewardship exists solely to prevent misuse and confusion and does not grant ownership or control of the Community Code or its content.

## Permitted Use

You may reference the Open Heat Initiative by name in a factual manner, including to state that your community uses or adapts the Open Heat Community Code.

## Prohibited Use

You may not present your project as the official Open Heat Initiative or use Initiative branding in a way that suggests endorsement or governance authority.

## Questions

If you are unsure whether a particular use is appropriate, please open an issue in this repository.
